Sign up

You can use the Airbnb website to find and book an experience for a private group. Private groups let you reserve all the spots in an experience on the specific date and time that it is offered.

On the Airbnb home page, enter your destination in the search box and then choose Experiences:

  1. Click Dates and enter your date (if you don’t know your date or it’s flexible, you can do this later).
  2. Click Guests and enter the number of guests.
  3. When you find the experience that you want to book, you can message the host to learn if they can host your group privately or customize the experience for your group.

Contact your Host or guest

Sending a message is usually the quickest and easiest way to resolve an issue with your listing or reservation. Or you could try calling them.
